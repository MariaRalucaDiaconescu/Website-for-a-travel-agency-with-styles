    Haarlem is a city situated in the province North Holland in the Netherlands. It belongs to the Amsterdam Metropolitan area, which is one of the regions in Europe with the highest population density. The Haarlem City Hall encompasses the residence of the local authorities. The city has the urban area and the metropolitan area. It has a population density of 5,528 inhabitants per km2. It is situated near the Zuid-Kennemerland National Park, on the river Spaarne. The city contains several many cultural places like museums and theaters.

    Roermond is a city in the province Limburg in the Netherlands. It is situated in the southeast of the country. It is a city with a very important cultural role, with many building and monuments which ensure this status. The city has a population of 957 inhabitants per km2. The river Maas is situated to the West of the province and Germany to the East. It houses two natural reserves, the Meinweg National Park and the Valley of the Leu.
